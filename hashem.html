More About Me

Hi there! I'm Hashem, and outside of my work, I have several hobbies that keep me inspired and energized. Here's a bit more about what I enjoy doing in my free time.

Camping Adventures

There's nothing quite like the great outdoors. I love camping and getting away from life. Whether it's hiking through the mountains or setting up camp by a lake, I’m always ready for the next adventure!

Creating Art

Art has always been a creative outlet for me. I enjoy sketching, painting, and even experimenting with digital art. It’s a way for me to express myself and unwind.

Video Games

I’m a big fan of video games, especially strategy games. There's nothing quite like solving puzzles, building strategies, or going on epic adventures, all from the comfort of my couch.

Hanging Out with Friends

Spending time with friends is something I always look forward to. Whether we're playing games, exploring new spots, or just relaxing, it's all about making memories.
